# Email Draft: Carlos Santana

To: csantana@sas.upenn.edu

Subject: Paper building on "What Is Language?"

Dear Professor Santana,

I hope you're well. I'm writing because I'm finishing a paper, "What do we mean by language?", for possible submission to *Language Sciences*. It begins from your 2016 *Ergo* paper as the closest precedent and tries to add a Boyd-style homeostatic property cluster (HPC) and projectibility apparatus to a more fine-grained map of the senses of language across the language sciences.

The central thought isn't that your pluralism is wrong, but that its three-ontology map can be made more diagnostic. When evidence about one sense of language is used to underwrite claims about another, the problem can be stated as a failure of projectibility across differently stabilized clusters.

I'd be grateful to send you the draft if you'd be willing to have a look. No pressure, of course. I mainly wanted to acknowledge the debt to your paper and ask whether the framing seems fair to you.

Best,

Brett
